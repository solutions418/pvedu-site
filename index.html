Welcome

Please note that I'm in the process of rebuilding this site. I apologize for any issues you might experience while this work is underway. Long story short, the old site was based on WordPress and had a database error that took it offline for a while. Thankfully, I have a full backup, so no data was lost. I just don't want to deal with WordPress anymore, as it requires a lot of maintenance to keep the site secure.

Welcome to PVeducation.com. This site is designed to teach you about solar electric systems of all different types.

I am currently in the solar electric industry, and a significant part of my work involves sharing knowledge with others. This is the foundation of this website—I want to share the practical knowledge I have gained over the years with everyone. This site is designed to be a straightforward guide to all things solar electric, and I am building it based on personal experience and practical knowledge. PVeducation.com is for solar installers, students, homeowners, and end users of all types. I hope you will find this site to be a valuable foundation of knowledge presented from a straightforward point of view. My goal is to cut through the hype and marketing from manufacturers to provide you with an unbiased perspective. I will be creating short, focused topics to make it easy to find and read the information you need. If you want a complete solar education, be sure to read everything. Please check back often, as new content will be added regularly.

Thanks!
